About Amber Alwardah

A Connoisseur's Choice

Established in 2023, Amber Alwardah is a premier import and export company based in the vibrant heart of Dubai. Our journey began with a simple passion: to source the world's finest quality dry fruits and share them with customers who appreciate exceptional taste and wholesome goodness.

Our curated selection focuses on the most sought-after natural treasures, including cashews, nutrient-rich almonds, vibrant pistachios, hearty walnuts and more. We believe that quality is paramount, which is why every product in our collection is carefully selected to ensure it meets our high standards for flavor and freshness.

The Goodness of Nature

Power-Packed in Every Bite

  • Almonds: Praised for their heart-healthy fats, almonds are also a fantastic source of Vitamin E and protein, making them perfect for maintaining energy and glowing skin.
  • Walnuts: Often called "brain food," walnuts are uniquely rich in Omega-3 fatty acids, which are essential for cognitive function and reducing inflammation.
  • Pistachios: These vibrant nuts are packed with antioxidants, fiber, and protein, making them a satisfying, lower-calorie option that supports good gut health.
  • Cashews: Known for their creamy texture, cashews are an excellent source of essential minerals like iron and magnesium, vital for energy production and bone health.
